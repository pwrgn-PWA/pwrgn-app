# PWRGEN App Installation Guide

## What You're Getting

This is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that creates an icon on your Android home screen to quickly access your PWRGEN website. It works just like a native app but doesn't require installation from the Play Store.

## Installation Steps (Android)

### Option 1: Using Chrome Browser (Recommended)

1. **Open Chrome** on your Android device
2. **Navigate to** the app URL (your hosting provider will give you this)
3. **Tap the menu icon** (three dots) in the top-right corner
4. **Select "Install app"** or **"Add to Home screen"**
5. **Confirm** the installation
6. The app icon will now appear on your home screen!

### Option 2: Using Firefox Browser

1. **Open Firefox** on your Android device
2. **Navigate to** the app URL
3. **Tap the menu icon** (three lines) at the bottom
4. **Select "Install"**
5. **Confirm** the installation
6. The app icon will appear on your home screen

### Option 3: Using Samsung Internet

1. **Open Samsung Internet** on your Android device
2. **Navigate to** the app URL
3. **Tap the menu icon** (three dots)

## How It Works

- When you tap the app icon, it opens your PWRGEN website in full-screen mode
- The app runs in "standalone" mode, hiding the browser UI
- Your website loads in a WebView container
- All features of your website work normally

## Troubleshooting

**"Install app" option not showing?**
- Make sure you're using a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, or Samsung Internet)
