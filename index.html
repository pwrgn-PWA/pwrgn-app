Access your website directly from your home screen

Install PWRGEN

Follow these steps to add PWRGEN to your home screen:

  1. Tap the menu icon (three dots) in the top-right corner
  2. Select "Add to Home screen" or "Create shortcut"
  3. Confirm the installation
  4. The PWRGEN icon will appear on your home screen

When you tap the icon, it will open your website in app mode.
