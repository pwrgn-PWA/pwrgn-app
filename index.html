Add PWRGEN to Home Screen

Follow these simple steps:

  1. Tap the menu icon (three dots) in the top-right corner of Chrome
  2. Select "Add to Home screen"
  3. Confirm by tapping "Add"
  4. Done! PWRGEN icon will appear on your home screen

When you tap the icon, it will open your PWRGEN website directly.
